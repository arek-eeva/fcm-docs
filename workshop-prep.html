01

Set up before you arrive

Essential~30 min

These are the things that, if they're not sorted, will eat into your build time on the day. Do them at home where you've got your passwords and a good connection.

02

Build your context folder

High value~45 min

Make one folder called something like "AI-OS-stuff" and drop everything below into it. This is what teaches your OS who you are on day one. Don't tidy or rename anything, just gather. The more you bring, the more it sounds like you from the first session. It all stays on your own laptop.

03

Export your AI's memory

High value~20 min

If you've used ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini for your business, it already knows a lot about you. There's a trick to get all of it out in one go: ask it to write a handover, the way you'd brief someone taking over your job. Paste the prompt below into each AI tool or project you've used, then save the answer into your context folder. This is the single biggest head start you can give yourself.

Paste this into your AI tool
I'm handing over our work to a new assistant who knows nothing about me or my business yet. Please write a complete handover document so they can pick up exactly where you left off.

Organise it under clear headings, and be specific. Use real names, numbers, dates, and examples wherever you can, not vague generalities. Cover:

1. My business: what it is, what I sell, who my customers are, where I operate.
2. Me: my name, my role, how I like to work, anything personal you've learned that's relevant.
3. My goals: what I'm working towards, and what matters most right now.
4. Ongoing work: projects and jobs in motion, and where each one is up to.
5. Key people: clients, team, suppliers, and the relationship with each.
6. My preferences: how I like things done, my tone and writing style, pet hates.
7. Decisions and rules: anything I've told you to always or never do.
8. Anything else a new assistant would need to not start from zero.

Write it as a clear document I can hand straight to someone else. Don't ask me questions first, just write the fullest handover you can from everything you already know.
