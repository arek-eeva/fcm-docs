The idea

The same story, told leaner

Here are two focused ways to make Rock to Market, both built on a single idea: be deliberate about where we film.

The first plan covered ten sites from quarry to customer. These two options carry the same story through fewer, carefully chosen locations, the ones that hold the most weight on screen. Filming less, but filming the right places, keeps the production tight: less travel, a shorter schedule, a leaner crew. That is what brings the cost down.

Both options are still board-grade. Both still give the CRH board the scale, the connection across the business and the gravity the room calls for. The difference between them is simply how much of the chain you want on screen.

Two options

Choose the scope that fits

Both keep the rock-to-market spine and the same finish. Option A holds to the four locations that carry the whole arc. Option B adds the concrete side of the business and a real customer site, so the chain reaches all the way to market.

The finish
