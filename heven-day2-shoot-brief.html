The setup

Keep it simple

  • Camera: one body, wide-angle lens, 4K, locked off on a tripod. Frame each interviewee wide enough to crop and punch in to them in the edit. No moves.
  • These are solo interviews (Jason, then Markus), so it's one person in frame at a time. Reset for each.
  • Audio: a lapel mic on the interviewee plus a safety track. Clean audio is the main thing.
  • Interviewer / prompt: sitting next to the camera on the light side, so the eyeline falls toward the camera and the key light.
  • Lighting: simple and soft, one key to one side. Don't overthink it.
  • Background: a clean navy or grey wall with a bit of depth (the talent are dressed to contrast it). Keep windows out of the background.
  • Photos: a few relaxed photos of the fund team, Mena, Ben and Melanie, together and individually, for the pitch deck and fund collateral.
The look we're after: relaxed, clean, nicely lit, shot wide so we crop later. Easy setup over elaborate.
