Getting unstuck.

Everyone hits a wall in the first weeks. Here's how to get past it without waiting on anyone.

An error popped up

Errors are normal. Don't panic.

Paste it straight back to your Chief of Staff, or take a screenshot and drop it in: "this came up, can you fix it?" Most of the time, your Chief of Staff can.

It's behaving strangely

The usual cause is a stale or contradictory file. Ask your Chief of Staff to check your CLAUDE.md and memory/RECENT.md for anything out of date or clashing, then fix the source.

Do less, check more.

Something needs the terminal

Now and then a job needs something installed or a command run. Your Chief of Staff hands you a line to paste into the terminal. Copy, paste, done. You don't need to understand it.

Claude Code wants updating

The app updates often. If something looks off or a feature's missing, update Claude Code first, then try again. Keep on top of it.

Still stuck

Ask your Chief of Staff to help you get unstuck before you call me. That's the muscle worth building.

If you're still stuck after that, call. We'll share screens and sort it. The goal is you never stay stuck for long.

Being stuck is part of it, not a sign you're doing it wrong. It passes fast.
