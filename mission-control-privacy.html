Privacy Policy — Five Castles Mission Control

Five Castles Pty Ltd (ABN 76 651 385 545), trading as Fifth Castle Media and EEVA. Last updated 9 June 2026.

About this application

Five Castles Mission Control is a private, internal operations dashboard used solely by Five Castles Pty Ltd. It is not offered to the public, has no external or third-party users, and is not a multi-tenant product.

Data we access

With the account owner's authorisation, the application reads accounting data from the business's own QuickBooks Online company on a read-only basis. This includes received payments, deposits, and bank-account balances. The application does not create, modify, or delete any data in QuickBooks.

How we use the data

The data is displayed to the business owner within the internal dashboard to show received payments and the current cash position. It is not used for any other purpose, and it is not used for marketing, profiling, or any automated decision-making affecting third parties.

Storage and security

The data is stored on the business's own computer and a private, access-controlled cloud backup owned by the business. Access credentials are held only on the local machine in a restricted-permission file and are never placed in shared or publicly accessible locations. Data is encrypted at rest (full-disk encryption) and in transit (TLS). The dashboard is reachable only over a private network, not the public internet. Access is limited to the single business owner.

Sharing

The data is not sold, shared with, or disclosed to any third party. There are no subprocessors.

Retention and deletion

Stored data may be deleted at any time. Authorisation for the application to access QuickBooks may be revoked at any time from the QuickBooks account's connected-apps settings, which immediately ends all access.
