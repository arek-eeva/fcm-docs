The setup

Keep it simple

  • Camera: one body, wide-angle lens, 4K, locked off on a tripod. Frame both side by side, wide enough to crop and punch in to each person in the edit. No moves.
  • Audio: two lapel mics, one per person, plus a safety track. Clean audio is the main thing.
  • Interviewer: Colette sits next to the camera on the light side, so the talent's eyeline falls toward the camera and the key light.
  • Lighting: simple and soft, one key to one side. Don't overthink it.
  • Background: HEVEN's navy or grey backdrop (the talent are dressed to contrast it). Clean, with a bit of depth, no window behind.
  • Photos: a few casual photos of the two of them, candid, while you're set up. Nothing formal.
The look we're after: relaxed, clean, nicely lit, shot wide so we crop later. Easy setup over elaborate.
