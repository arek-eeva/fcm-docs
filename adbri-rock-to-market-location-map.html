The route, rock to market

Limestone is shipped Klein Point → Birkenhead aboard the Yampu. Cement is then shipped Birkenhead → Port Melbourne by a separate carrier, and distributed across Victoria by road to concrete plants, masonry and customer projects.

South Australia

  • Klein Point: dawn opening over the quarry, plus the Yampu loading by conveyor (a morning window). Drone.
  • Birkenhead: the cement plant and the Yampu unloading, with approach shots from across the water. Full day for the site's scale. Drone.

Crew based in Adelaide, with an overnight near the quarry for the early start.

Victoria

  • Port Melbourne, Yarraville, South Wharf, Campbellfield: a metropolitan cluster of half-day visits.
  • Echuca: a half-day, filmed by a local regional crew, with no Melbourne travel or overnight.
  • Customer projects: one metro, one regional, selected closer to filming.

Timing

Filming and post wrapped through August, ahead of the CRH board visit in October.
