Grievance and Complaints Policy

Applies to: employees and subcontractors  ·  Version 1.0  ·  Last updated 17 June 2026
Five Castles Pty Ltd (ABN 76 651 385 545), trading as Fifth Castle Media

Our approach

If something is not right, we want to know early. This policy explains how to raise any work-related concern, whether it is about safety, conduct, pay, or how a job is run, and what will happen when you do. Raising a concern is always treated as a constructive thing to do.

The principles we follow

  • Taken seriously. Every concern raised in good faith is listened to.
  • Fair. Everyone involved is treated fairly and given a chance to be heard before any decision.
  • Confidential. Information is shared only with those who need it to deal with the matter.
  • No victimisation. No one is disadvantaged for raising a concern or supporting someone who has.

How to raise a concern

Step 1: Raise it directly, if you are comfortable

Many issues are resolved quickly by talking to the person involved. This step is optional and only if you feel safe to do it.

Step 2: Raise it with Arek

Contact Arek Rainczuk at info@fivecastles.com.au or on 0467 676 205. Set out what happened, who was involved, and what you would like to see done. We will acknowledge it, look into it fairly, and let you know the outcome.

Step 3: Go outside the business

If the concern involves Arek, or you would rather not raise it internally, you can go to an external body:

  • Fair Work Commission: fwc.gov.au
  • Fair Work Ombudsman (pay and entitlements): fairwork.gov.au, 13 13 94
  • Victorian Equal Opportunity and Human Rights Commission: humanrights.vic.gov.au, 1300 292 153
  • WorkSafe Victoria (health and safety): 13 23 60

For concerns about discrimination, bullying or sexual harassment, see the Equal Opportunity, Anti-Discrimination and Bullying Policy and the Sexual Harassment Prevention Policy and Plan, which set out additional reporting paths.

Review

This policy is reviewed at least once a year, and sooner if our work or the law changes.
