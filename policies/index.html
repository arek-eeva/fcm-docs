Our policies

These are the policies everyone who works with Fifth Castle Media is expected to read and follow, whether you are an employee or a subcontractor. They set out how we keep people safe, how we treat each other and our clients, and what is required to work under the Fifth Castle Media name.

Choose a policy below to open it. If anything here is unclear, ask Arek. The policies are reviewed regularly and updated as the business and the law change.
